## 4. Data Security & Protection

### 4.1 Security Measures

Kohza implements industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards**
- Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS protocol)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Secure authentication and access controls
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning
- Intrusion detection and prevention systems
- Firewalls and network security

**Administrative Safeguards**
- Restricted access to personal information
- Employee confidentiality agreements
- Data access logging and monitoring
- Incident response and breach protocols
- Regular security training for staff

**Physical Safeguards**
- Secure data centers with limited access
- Surveillance and monitoring systems
- Backup power and disaster recovery systems
- Secure disposal of physical records

### 4.2 Limitation of Security

**No guarantee of complete security.** While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against all security breaches, hacking, or unauthorized access.

You acknowledge the inherent risks of digital data transmission and storage.

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide services and fulfill contractual obligations
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
- Pursue legitimate business interests

**Retention periods vary by data type:**
- Account information: Duration of service + 3 years
- Payment records: As required by tax/accounting regulations
- Enforcement data: Duration of enforcement period + 2 years
- Analytics data: Up to 24 months
- Backup data: Up to 90 days

Upon request or account termination, we will delete or de-identify your personal information, except where legal obligations require retention.
